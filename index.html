My Sports Life

One of the biggest parts of my life is sports, especially competitive wrestling. Wrestling has taught me discipline, mental toughness, and how to push through challenges even when things get difficult. In addition to wrestling, I also train in boxing and mixed martial arts, which helps me build strength, endurance, and confidence. Training regularly requires commitment and consistency, but I enjoy the process of improving every day. Being involved in combat sports has helped me develop a strong work ethic that I apply to other parts of my life as well. Through these sports, I’ve learned the value of perseverance, focus, and self-control.

Philosophy & Reflection

Another interest I have is philosophy, which I enjoy learning about and thinking about in my free time. Philosophy helps me understand different perspectives on life, ethics, and human behavior. I like exploring questions about purpose, discipline, and what it means to live a meaningful life. Thinking about philosophical ideas also helps me reflect on my own goals and values. It connects well with sports because both require mental strength and self-awareness. Studying philosophy encourages me to think deeply and question ideas rather than just accepting things at the surface level.

Business & Leadership

At school, I stay involved in activities like DECA and other clubs that help me develop leadership, teamwork, and communication skills. Outside of school, I also spend time helping with my dad’s business and different business projects. These projects sometimes require traveling to different locations, which has given me the opportunity to see how real businesses operate and learn about entrepreneurship at a young age. Being involved in business has helped me understand responsibility, problem-solving, and the importance of hard work. When I’m not busy with school, training, or business projects, I enjoy spending time with my family. I have two siblings and a dog, so our house is always active and full of energy. Balancing sports, school, business, and family helps me stay motivated and continue growing in different areas of my life.
